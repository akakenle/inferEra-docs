## Overview

- **Unified Interface, Seamless Migration**: Compatible with OpenAI SDK, allowing for smooth switching with zero code changes.
- **Multi-Interface Compatibility**: Supports various compatible format interfaces including OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic.
- **Comprehensive Model Capabilities**: Full coverage of mainstream models for text, image/video generation, vector embedding, TTS, and more to meet diverse scene requirements.
- **Flexible Billing, Pay-As-You-Go**: No membership fees, no monthly subscriptions; costs grow in sync with your business.

## Common Features and Scenarios

- **Development Tools:** Use in development tools such as [Claude Code](https://docs.aihubmix.com/en/api/Claude-Code), [OpenCode](https://docs.aihubmix.com/en/api/Opencode), [Cline](https://docs.aihubmix.com/en/api/Cline), and [Gemini Cli](https://docs.aihubmix.com/en/api/Cline).
