After registering and logging in, click your avatar to enter the admin panel.  
Select External Links and configure the two fields under Open API.  
- Enter the [Key from our site](https://aihubmix.com/token) in the right-hand field.  
- Enter the following in the left-hand field:
```
https://aihubmix.com/v1
```
